## Derived Reference Files

- `software-architecture-patterns.md`: original compact rubric for layered, event-driven, microkernel, microservices, and space-based architecture tradeoffs.
- `clean-architecture-principles.md`: original compact rubric for dependency direction, boundaries, component principles, details discipline, services, tests, and packaging enforcement.
- `architecture-best-practices.md`: practical review heuristics synthesized from the sources.
- `pattern-fit-rubric.md`: decision rubric for accepting or rejecting candidate architecture styles.
